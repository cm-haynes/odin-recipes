Pumpkin Pie


Description

This is a delicious, and spicy recipe for pumpkin pie that is almost better cold than hot. If you want a more docile pie then feel free to leave out the cayenne, but be sure to try it first.



Ingredients


Filling

  • 16oz of pureed pumpkin
  • One cup of white sugar
  • 8oz of heavy cream
  • Cayenne pepper to taste
  • 1 tsp clove

Crust

  • 1 cup white flour
  • 8oz butter softened
  • 1tsp salt

Steps

  1. After placing the flour in a bowl cut in the butter
  2. Pat the crust into a pie pan and cook at 350 for five minutes
  3. Remove the crust from the oven and set aside to cool
  4. Mix the pumpkin, sugar and heavy cream in a bowl and whip until cream
  5. Add cayenne and clove slowly to taste
  6. Pour the filling into the baked crust and bake at 375 for 15 to 20 minutes
  7. Remove pie when a toothpick comes out clean and set aside to cool
