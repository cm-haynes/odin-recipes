Lasagna



Description


You have made an excellent choice. This recipe for lasagna is as easy as it is delicious. This seven layer lasagna starts with cheese and then adds noodles sauce and then you do it all again. Over and over for every delicous layer. A quick toss in the oven and you have a masterpiece meant for the whole family!

Please make sure to follow all the directions precisely and you will have a delicous meal and leftovers to share.


Ingredients

  • One box of lasagna noodles
  • 8oz of Ricotta cheese
  • 8oz of Cottage cheese
  • 8oz of parmesan cheese
  • 4oz can of tomato paste
  • 8oz can of tomato sauce


Steps

  1. Boil a pot of water and add lasagna noodles until soft
  2. Mix Ricotta and cottage chesse in a bowl and add salt to taste
  3. Mix tomato paste and tomato sauce in a small bowl
  4. oat the bottom of a 13in x 9in pan with cheese mixture
  5. Add one layer of lasagna noodles above cheese mixture
  6. Add a thin layer of the tomato sauce on top of noodles
  7. Add another layer of noodles topped with another layer of cheese
  8. Continue layering until all the noodles are used
  9. Coat the final layer of noodles in permesan cheese
  10. Bake in an oven preheated to 350 degrees for 15 to 20 minutes
  11. Remove from oven when golden brown and let cool before eating
