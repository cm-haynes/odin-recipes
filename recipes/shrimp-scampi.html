Shrimp Scampi



Description

This delicious dish was inspired in the Italian countryside. The oregano makes this dish so fresh and lively and the bigger the shrimp the better for the aesthetics. Feel free to experience with lemon, some love it, others hate it.



Ingredients

  • One box of linquini noodles
  • 4oz of cream
  • Oregano to taste
  • 1tsp of salt
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Bring 8 cups of water to a boil salting lightly
  2. Add noodles and cook for 10 minutes or until soft
  3. Remove noodles from pot, add part of the oil, and mix
  4. In a sautepan warm cream and add salt and oregano
  5. Bring 8 cups of water to a boil and add shrimp
  6. Cook shrimp for approximately three minutes or until bright red
  7. Remove from pot and set aside
  8. Stir noodles and shrimp into cream mixture making sure everything is even covered
